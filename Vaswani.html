The Transformer Architecture

The Transformer's key innovation was the **self-attention mechanism**. It allowed the model to process all words in a sentence simultaneously and weigh their importance relative to each other, overcoming the limitations of older, sequential models like RNNs.

Unprecedented Influence

  • Became the foundational architecture for most modern Large Language Models (LLMs).
  • Directly enabled the creation of models like **GPT** and **BERT**.
  • Applications have expanded beyond NLP into computer vision, drug discovery, and more.

Career & Further Learning

After his influential work at Google, Vaswani continued to be a leader in the field, co-founding AI startups **Adept AI Labs** and later **Essential AI** to further push the boundaries of artificial intelligence.
