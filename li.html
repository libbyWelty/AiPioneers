From Immigrant to Innovator

  • ➤Immigrated to the U.S. from China at age 16, facing significant financial and language barriers.
  • ➤Excelled academically, earning a scholarship to Princeton University for her undergraduate degree in physics.
  • ➤Received her Ph.D. from the California Institute of Technology (Caltech) in 2005, focusing on computer vision and neuroscience.

The ImageNet Revolution

In the mid-2000s, AI models lacked enough data to learn effectively. Dr. Li's solution was radical: create a massive, meticulously labeled dataset of images to teach computers how to "see."

  • ➤Led the creation of **ImageNet**, a database with over **14 million images** across 20,000+ categories.
  • ➤This project provided the "fuel" for deep learning models, sparking the modern AI boom.
  • ➤The annual ImageNet Challenge became the benchmark for computer vision progress.
